About Our Show



Burning Rock Radio is a fictional audiobook podcast about Ivy Romeo, a grad student living in the creepy coastal town of Burning Rock, Washington. Follow Ivy as she dodges monsters and searches for the truth about her missing friend.


Tune in Wednesdays for new episodes!
